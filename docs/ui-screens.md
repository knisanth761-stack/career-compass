# 🖥 Career Compass — UI Screens Plan (MVP)

This document defines the core user interface screens for the MVP and what each screen must display.  
Goal: Keep the UI **progress-first, simple, and motivating**.

---

## 🏠 1) Dashboard (Home Screen)

### 🎯 Purpose
Give users an instant view of:
- Today’s status
- Current momentum
- What to do next
- Companies within reach

This is the screen users should open daily.

---

### 📊 Sections

#### A) Today’s Summary Card
Shows:
- Today’s date
- “Have you logged progress today?” status
- Quick button: **Log Today**

---

#### B) Streak & Momentum Panel
Displays:
- Current streak 🔥
- Longest streak
- Momentum Score (0–100) with progress bar

Breakdown tooltip:
- Consistency
- Effort
- Difficulty
- Coverage

---

#### C) Quick Next Actions
Shows 1–3 suggestions:
- “Solve 3 medium problems today”
- “Finish Sliding Window topic”
- “Study 45 minutes”

Generated by engines.

---

#### D) Company Reach Preview
Shows top 3 companies:
- Badge: Ready / Close / Long Term
- Small progress indicator

Button:
👉 “View All Companies”

---

## 🛤 2) Roadmap Screen

### 🎯 Purpose
Show structured learning path.

---

### 📊 Layout

#### A) Track Selector
Tabs:
- DSA & Core CS
- Web Dev
- Data/ML
- Cybersecurity

---

#### B) Topic List (Checklist Style)
Each topic shows:
- Status: Not started / In progress / Done
- Difficulty level
- Estimated days
- Prerequisites lock indicator

---

#### C) Highlight Next Topic
System suggests:
👉 “Best next topic to learn”

---

## 📝 3) Daily Log Screen

### 🎯 Purpose
Allow users to record progress quickly.

---

### 📊 Inputs

#### A) DSA Section
Fields:
- Problems solved (number)
- Easy / Medium / Hard counts
- Topics practiced

---

#### B) Study Time Section
Fields:
- Total minutes spent
- Core CS time (optional)

---

#### C) Projects Section
Fields:
- Minutes spent on projects
- Tasks completed

---

#### D) Notes Section
Optional free text.

---

#### E) Submit Button
Saves daily log.

---

## 🎯 4) Company Reach Meter Screen

### 🎯 Purpose
Provide realistic motivation based on progress.

---

### 📊 Layout

#### A) Filter Tabs
- Ready Now
- Close to Reach
- Long Term

---

#### B) Company Cards
Each card shows:
- Company name
- Tier badge
- Completion percentage

---

#### C) Requirement Breakdown

##### Matched (Green)
- Requirements satisfied

##### Missing (Red)
- Requirements not yet met

---

#### D) Next Steps Suggestions
Auto-generated actions:
- Solve X problems
- Complete Y topics
- Improve momentum score

---

## 📈 5) Analytics Screen (Future Phase)

Will show:
- Weekly activity chart
- Topic coverage chart
- Score trend graph

(Not part of MVP)

---

## 🎨 UI Philosophy

The interface should:
- Be minimal and distraction-free
- Focus on progress metrics
- Provide realistic motivation
- Encourage consistency through visual feedback

---

## 📌 MVP Screen Priority Order

Build in this order:

1) Dashboard
2) Daily Log
3) Company Reach Meter
4) Roadmap

---

## 🧠 Summary

The UI should always answer:
- What should I do today?
- How am I progressing?
- What companies am I close to?
- What is my next step?
